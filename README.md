🧠 OIBSIP_Data_Analytics_Task1

📊 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on Retail Sales Data

---

📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on performing Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on retail sales data to uncover patterns, trends, and actionable insights. The dataset contains transaction-level information such as customer demographics, product categories, and sales values.

The goal of this analysis is to support data-driven business decision-making.

---

🎯 Objectives

- Analyze retail sales data
- Identify trends and seasonal patterns
- Understand customer behavior
- Perform data visualization
- Provide actionable business insights

---

📂 Dataset Information

- Total Records: 1000
- Total Columns: 9
- Key Features:
  - Customer ID
  - Age
  - Gender
  - Product Category
  - Quantity
  - Price per Unit
  - Total Amount
  - Date

---

🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib (Data Visualization)
- Seaborn (Statistical Visualization)
- Jupyter Notebook

---

🧹 Data Cleaning & Preprocessing

- No missing values were identified in the dataset
- Data types were verified and corrected
- Converted date column into datetime format
- Extracted new features (Year, Month) for analysis

---

📈 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

📅 Monthly Sales Trend

- Sales peaked in May (~₹52,000)
- Lowest sales recorded in September (~₹24,000)
- Indicates strong seasonal patterns

👉 Recommendation:
Prepare inventory and marketing campaigns in advance for peak months. Offer discounts during low-performing months.

---

🛍️ Sales by Product Category

- Electronics: ₹1,56,905
- Clothing: ₹1,55,580
- Beauty: ₹1,43,515

👉 Insight: Balanced category performance
👉 Recommendation: Promote Beauty category through targeted offers

---

👩‍🦰 Sales by Gender

- Female: ₹2,32,840
- Male: ₹2,23,160

👉 Insight: Almost equal contribution
👉 Recommendation: Focus on gender-neutral marketing strategies

---

🎯 Sales by Age Group

- Highest spending: Age 30–55
- Lower spending: Age 18–25

👉 Recommendation:
Target premium products to 30–55 group
Introduce budget-friendly options for younger customers

---

🏆 Top 5 Customers

- Highest transaction value: ₹2,000

👉 Recommendation:
Introduce VIP loyalty programs for high-value customers

---

🔥 Correlation Analysis

- Strong correlation: Price per Unit ↔ Total Amount (0.85)
- Moderate correlation: Quantity ↔ Total Amount (0.37)

👉 Recommendation:
Promote premium products and bundle offers

---

💡 Final Business Recommendations

1. Plan inventory based on seasonal demand
2. Boost underperforming categories with offers
3. Focus on high-value customer segments
4. Introduce loyalty programs
5. Strengthen premium pricing strategy
